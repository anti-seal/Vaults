How do ANTISEAL's Sealed Vaults work?
Deposit Liquidity Pool (LP) tokens into a Sealed Vault to receive xANTI.
We currently have vaults which accept ANTISEAL-ETH, USDT-ETH, USDC-ETH, DAI-ETH and WBTC-ETH.
In the near future, more vaults such as ROT-ETH, LINK-ETH will be added.

What is xANTI and what can I do with it?
xANTI is ANTISEAL's governance token. It will be used to allow for proposals to be created and voted on, which will continuously determine the future of ANTISEAL. All xANTI holders will have a say on whether or not a proposal is passed.
The website for proposals is currently in development and will be released in the future. For now, if you want a say in ANTISEAL'S future, feel free to join our Telegram or @ us on Twitter.

Will there be a dollar value associated with xANTI?
Supply smoothing is intended to reflect changes in demand of xANTI.
This will allow the scarce ownership of the network to be maintained.
After a rebase, all holders own the same share of the network as they did before the rebase.
The network is simply divided into a new number of shares.

Through the rebase functionality, we plan on re-designing xANTI to be pegged at $1.00 USD.
It can still go under or over this $ value but each day, a rebase will occur, changing the supply which in turn can change the value.
